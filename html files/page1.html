Nashville Users, Engaged Sessions, and Key Events in relation to Web Sources & Temperature

Project Overview

Pull data from Google Analytics, investigated the relationship between Temperature and Website Traffic over time and the key reasons that bring more customers to the website. The graphs are interactive. When you click Daily, the daily results will show up, and when you click Monthly, monthly data will show up. For the right-hand side legend bar, double click to select just one legend, and double click to unselect. If you want to hide one legend, single click it. Moreover, you can magnify an area on the graph by dragging a window on it. Finally, click the little house symbol on the top right to restore the original graph. (If you view it on your phone, please swipe left to see the entire graph. You can drag the end of x-axis to the left to narrow the graph.)
