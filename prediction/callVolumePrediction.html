Call Volume Prediction

Project Overview

Utilized historical call volumes as training data to predict future volumes using fixed-effect linear and polynomial models. The model initially included 150 parameters, accounting for factors such as lagged weather, historical call data, market spending, and time. Applied LASSO (Least Absolute Shrinkage Selection Operator) to eliminate unnecessary parameters and compared the mean squared error on test data across both models.
